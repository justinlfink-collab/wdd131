Good Design Builds Better Experiences

Web design should help people quickly understand content and move through a page with ease. Strong design choices improve readability, highlight important information, and create a more enjoyable user experience.

Effective pages use a limited set of fonts, a simple color palette, and consistent spacing. These choices make a page feel organized and professional instead of crowded or confusing.

Design Tip

High contrast between text and background improves readability and accessibility. Clean spacing and consistent alignment also help users focus on what matters most.

  • Use no more than two or three fonts.
  • Keep the color palette simple and consistent.
  • Use whitespace to reduce clutter.
  • Create clear visual hierarchy with size and weight.
  • Check contrast for accessibility compliance.
  • Provide clear feedback and readable layouts.

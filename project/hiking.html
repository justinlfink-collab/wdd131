Hiking Adventures

Find trails near Salem Utah that offer scenic views, mountain climbs, and family-friendly outdoor experiences.

Featured Hiking Locations

Loafer Mountain Trail

Loafer Mountain offers panoramic views of Utah Valley and is a favorite for hikers wanting a rewarding mountain climb.

  • Best for experienced hikers
  • Mountain and valley views
  • Good for summer and fall

Spanish Fork Peak

This trail provides a challenging route with beautiful alpine scenery and a strong sense of adventure.

  • Steeper trail experience
  • Scenic overlook opportunities
  • Popular local destination

Payson Lakes Trail

Payson Lakes Trail is a more relaxing option with lake views, forest surroundings, and a family-friendly environment.

  • Good for families
  • Great picnic area nearby
  • Easy access from the road

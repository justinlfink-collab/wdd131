Camping Destinations

Explore campgrounds and lakeside locations near Salem Utah for weekend getaways and peaceful outdoor time.

Featured Camping Locations

Payson Lakes Campground

Payson Lakes is one of the most well-known camping destinations in the area, offering lake views and a forest setting.

  • Beautiful lakeside scenery
  • Popular for weekend camping
  • Great for families and groups

Maple Lake Campground

Maple Lake provides a quiet and refreshing mountain camping experience with cooler temperatures and scenic surroundings.

  • Mountain atmosphere
  • Peaceful camping experience
  • Good access to nearby trails
